## 5  How We Use Personal Data  

1. **Core Functionality** — To unlock paid features such as unlimited tasks, Crazy Bubble mode, minimal notifications, and automatic icon switching.  
2. **Diagnostics & Security** — To detect crashes, prevent subscription fraud, and protect Service integrity.  
3. **Legal Compliance** — To meet tax, accounting, and statutory obligations.  
4. **Optional Marketing** — To send low-volume product news if you opt in.  
5. **Research & Development** — To improve or develop new features, including potential AI functionality.

We do **not** use automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you.
